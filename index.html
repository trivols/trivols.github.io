About Us

We are a club at the University of Tennessee that trains and races sprint and olympic triathlons. We have something to offer for the beginner to the experienced triathelete. Join us!

We train

We practice weekly, focusing primarily on swimming, biking, and running. We throw in some cross-training as well.

We race

We race as part of the Southeast Collegiate Triathlon Conference, racing in several collegiate races per year as well as a few local races in the spring and summer.

We have fun

Between competing in Ragnar relay events, competing in local races, and volunteering with some of our favorite outdoor organizations, we find ways to bond as a team while giving back to our awesome tri community.

Since 2006, the TriVols have been a training ground for triathletes to compete at the Collegiate level in the Southeast and against the top teams in the nation at Collegiate Nationals. More than just competition, the TriVols offer a fun and inclusive atmosphere for students at the University of Tennessee to train, as well as be involved in the triathlon community in East Tennessee. We do social events as well, be on the look out for awesome events in the weekly email! Interested in joining? Contact us below.

How to join

We have two options for to you to join the team! A competitive and non-competitive option.

Competitive

- Dues $70 for semester, $120 for year - Travel with Team for Conference Races, team covers travel fees - Must have USAT ID - Come to Practices - Race fees covered by you, race discounts for conference races (if available)

Non-Competitive

- Dues $40 for semester, $70 for year - Come out for a Non-Conference race with the team - Come to Practices - Race fees covered by you
